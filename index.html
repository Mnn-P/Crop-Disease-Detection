Abstract

The study investigates the application of federated learning (FL) in crop disease detection, using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Vision Transformers (ViTs). We utilize the PlantVillage dataset to train models that detect crop diseases in various plant leaves, preserving user data privacy and security.
