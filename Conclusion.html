Conclusion

Federated Learning proves to be a valuable tool for crop disease detection, enabling decentralized model training while preserving data privacy. CNN-based models like ResNet50 demonstrate high accuracy and efficiency, making them suitable for this task. Future research should focus on optimizing FL implementations to handle data heterogeneity and improve communication between clients.
