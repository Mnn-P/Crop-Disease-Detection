Conclusion

The use of machine learning technologies, in particular those based on Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Vision Transformers (ViTs), for crop disease classification is an evolving field. In this research, we explored the application of federated learning (FL) in this context, a new paradigm that has been underexplored in this field despite its numerous advantages regarding the security of user data and the preservation of confidentiality of sensitive data.
